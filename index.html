I am interested in a wide range of topics in theoretical astrophysics. Right now I mainly study the formation and long-term evolution of accretion disks around young (Class 0/I/II) stars, and how that impacts the formation of stars and planets.

I work with both pencil-and-paper theory and computation, and strive to achive some synergy between them by connecting realistic simulations of complex phenomena with simple, predictive analytic theories.
